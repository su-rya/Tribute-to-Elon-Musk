ABOUT TESLA SPACEX SOLARCITY THE BORING COMPANY

"Solar will be the single largest source of electricity"

SolarCity Corporation is a subsidiary of Tesla, Inc. that specializes in solar energy services and is headquartered in San Mateo, California.

SolarCity markets, manufactures, and installs residential and commercial solar panels and solar roofs in the US. In 2016, the company was acquired by Tesla, Inc. and now also offers energy storage services through Tesla
